# Methodology

## CRISP-DM Methodology

This project was developed following the **CRISP-DM (Cross-Industry Standard Process for Data Mining)** methodology, a widely recognized framework for Business Intelligence and Data Analytics projects.

The methodology provides a structured approach for transforming business problems into analytical solutions.

---

# 1. Business Understanding

The first phase consisted of identifying TOFTAL Technologies' business needs and defining the objectives of the Decision Support System.

The main goal was to improve strategic decision-making by providing managers with reliable and interactive business indicators.

---

# 2. Data Understanding

Business data were collected from the company's operational environment.

The available data included:

- Sales
- Customers
- Products
- Promotions
- Vendors

An exploratory analysis was performed to understand the structure and quality of the available data.

---

# 3. Data Preparation

The data preparation phase included:

- Data cleaning
- Missing value handling
- Data transformation
- Data integration
- KPI calculation
- Preparation of analytical tables

This phase ensured that the dataset was suitable for Business Intelligence analysis.

---

# 4. Data Modeling

A relational data model was designed to organize business information efficiently.

Relationships were created between business entities such as:

- Sales
- Customers
- Products
- Promotions
- Vendors

The model supports interactive filtering and analytical reporting in Power BI.

---

# 5. Dashboard Development

Interactive dashboards were developed using Microsoft Power BI.

The dashboards provide:

- Executive KPIs
- Sales analysis
- Customer analytics
- Product performance
- Sales channel analysis
- Profitability analysis

Interactive filters and custom visualizations improve data exploration and decision-making.

---

# 6. Business Evaluation

The final phase focused on interpreting analytical results and formulating business recommendations.

The developed dashboards enable decision-makers to monitor business performance, identify opportunities and support strategic planning.

---

# Why CRISP-DM?

The CRISP-DM methodology provides a structured framework that connects business objectives with analytical solutions.

Its iterative approach ensures that Business Intelligence projects remain aligned with organizational needs while delivering actionable insights.
