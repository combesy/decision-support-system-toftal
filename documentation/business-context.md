# Business Context

## Company Overview

TOFTAL Technologies is an e-commerce company that generates and manages a large volume of operational data through its daily business activities. These data include sales transactions, customers, products, promotions and vendors.

As business operations expanded, the company required a centralized Business Intelligence solution capable of transforming raw operational data into meaningful information that supports strategic decision-making.

---

# Business Problem

Despite having access to valuable business data, decision-makers encountered several challenges:

- Commercial data were dispersed across different sources.
- Performance monitoring relied on manual reporting.
- Customer purchasing behavior was difficult to analyze.
- Product profitability was not easily measurable.
- Promotional campaign performance was difficult to evaluate.
- Managers lacked real-time indicators to support strategic decisions.

These limitations reduced the company's ability to react quickly to business opportunities and optimize commercial performance.

---

# Project Objectives

The main objective of this project was to design and implement a Business Intelligence Decision Support System capable of transforming operational data into actionable insights.

More specifically, the project aimed to:

- Centralize business data for decision-making.
- Develop interactive Power BI dashboards.
- Monitor key business performance indicators (KPIs).
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
- Evaluate product profitability.
- Measure promotional campaign performance.
- Support strategic and operational decision-making.

---

# Expected Business Benefits

The proposed solution provides several benefits for decision-makers:

- Better visibility of commercial performance.
- Faster access to strategic information.
- Improved customer analysis.
- Better monitoring of promotional campaigns.
- Identification of the most profitable products.
- Support for data-driven business decisions.

---

# Scope of the Project

The Decision Support System focuses on the analysis of commercial activities through several complementary perspectives, including:

- Executive KPI monitoring
- Sales performance analysis
- Customer analytics
- Product performance
- Sales channel analysis
- Profitability analysis
- Promotional campaign evaluation

The project was developed following the CRISP-DM methodology and implemented using Microsoft Power BI as the Business Intelligence platform.
