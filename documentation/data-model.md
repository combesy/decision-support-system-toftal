# Data Model

## Overview

The data model was designed to organize TOFTAL Technologies' e-commerce data into a structured analytical model suitable for Business Intelligence reporting.

The model connects key business entities such as sales, products, customers, promotions and vendors.

---

## Main Tables

### Sales

The sales table contains transactional information related to customer purchases, including sales amounts, quantities, dates, products and related commercial information.

### Products

The products table contains product-related information such as product names, categories and product identifiers.

### Customers

The customers table contains information related to customer profiles and purchasing behavior.

### Promotions

The promotions table supports the analysis of promotional campaigns and their contribution to business performance.

### Vendors

The vendors table contains information related to vendors, sales actors or operational contributors involved in the sales process.

---

## Analytical Purpose

The data model supports several types of analysis:

- Sales performance analysis
- Product performance analysis
- Customer behavior analysis
- Promotion performance analysis
- Sales channel monitoring
- Profitability analysis
- KPI reporting

---

## Business Intelligence Value

A well-structured data model enables interactive reporting, reliable KPI calculation and consistent analysis across dashboards.

It also supports filtering, cross-analysis and decision-making through Power BI visualizations.
