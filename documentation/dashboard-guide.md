# Dashboard Guide

## Overview

This document describes the main Power BI dashboards developed for the Decision Support System project.

The dashboards were designed to help decision-makers monitor business performance, analyze sales results and identify key commercial insights.

---

## Home / Executive Dashboard

The Home Dashboard provides a global overview of TOFTAL Technologies' commercial performance.

It includes strategic KPIs and visual analyses such as:

- Total Revenue
- Total Margin
- Margin Rate
- Quantity Sold
- Sales Target
- Target Gap
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Revenue by Product Category
- Top Performing Products
- Pareto Analysis
- Sales Channel Distribution
- Product Performance Analysis

---

## Products & Customers

This dashboard focuses on product and customer analysis.

It helps identify:

- Best-selling products
- High-value customers
- Customer purchasing behavior
- Product performance patterns

---

## Profitability

This dashboard focuses on profitability analysis.

It supports the evaluation of:

- Product margins
- Category profitability
- Revenue contribution
- Commercial performance

---

## Sales Channels

This dashboard analyzes sales distribution by channel.

It helps compare the performance of different commercial channels and identify the most effective ones.

---

## Custom Tooltip

A custom tooltip was designed to provide additional contextual information when users interact with dashboard visuals.

This improves the user experience by giving decision-makers more detailed information without overcrowding the main dashboard.
