# Solution Architecture

## Decision Support System Architecture

The Business Intelligence solution was designed following a decision support architecture that transforms operational data into strategic business information.

The architecture integrates data collection, preparation, analytical modeling and interactive visualization within Microsoft Power BI.

# Main Components

## Operational Data Sources

The solution integrates operational business data including:

- Sales
- Customers
- Products
- Promotions
- Vendors

These data constitute the foundation of the analytical system.

---

## Data Preparation

Before analysis, the data were prepared through several operations:

- Data cleaning
- Data transformation
- Data integration
- KPI calculation
- Quality verification

This phase ensured data consistency and reliability.

---

## Business Intelligence Layer

The analytical layer was implemented using Microsoft Power BI.

It includes:

- Data model
- Business measures
- KPIs
- Interactive dashboards
- Business visualizations

---

## Decision Support Layer

The final layer provides strategic information for decision-makers through interactive dashboards and analytical reports.

Managers can monitor:

- Commercial performance
- Customer behavior
- Product performance
- Sales channels
- Profitability
- Promotional effectiveness

---

# Architecture Benefits

The proposed architecture enables:

- Centralized reporting
- Reliable business indicators
- Interactive analysis
- Faster decision-making
- Better monitoring of commercial performance
